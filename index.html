About Me

I am a staunch believer in the dissemination of knowledge to the public in a way that respects global diversity. For this reason, I am passionate about affordable education and accessible research. I hold a TEFL/TESOL certification and have worked as an educator, primarily with undergraduate students, in a variety of capacities. I also have three years of experience working in the academic publishing industry and am a pragmatic proponent of open access initiatives. In 2015, I obtained a BA in English with minors in Political Science and Professional Writing and a MA in Anglophone Mondernities in Literature and Culture in 2019. Thanks for checking out my portfolio!

Electoral Reform in Sri Lanka

I was a coauthor on a report the Politics research practice at Verité Research prepared for Transparency International Sri Lanka. The report examined Sri Lanka's electoral reform options, and the potential advantages and drawbacks different systems would have on Sri Lanka's democratic representation.
